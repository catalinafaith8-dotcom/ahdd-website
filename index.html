Meet Your Doctors

The Last Dentist
You'll Ever Need to Find

Drs. Shawn & David Matian  ·  Agoura Hills, CA

Most people don't love going to the dentist. We get it — and we built this practice specifically to change that. Whether you haven't been in years, are nervous about a procedure, or just want a smile you're genuinely proud of, we meet you exactly where you are.

As third-generation dentists with over 20 years of experience and 15,000+ patients treated, we have seen almost everything. More importantly, we know how to make you feel comfortable, informed, and confident in your care — from the first call to the final result.
