Getting here from Calabasas

From Calabasas, the office is a short drive west on the 101 — typically six minutes outside of rush hour. Patients coming from The Commons, Old Town, or the Las Virgenes / Malibu Canyon corridor all use the same route.

  • From The Commons at Calabasas: take the 101 west for about 4 miles; exit Reyes Adobe Road (#34); turn right on Roadside Drive.
  • From Old Town Calabasas: head west on Calabasas Road / Agoura Road for approximately 5 miles; Roadside Drive is on the right.
  • From the Calabasas Hills / Mulholland side: take Las Virgenes Road north to the 101 west, then exit Reyes Adobe.

Free on-site parking. Elevator access to the second-floor suite.

Quick answers

Questions from Calabasas patients

How long is the drive from Calabasas to your office?

Typically six minutes westbound on the 101 outside of commute hours. The office is two signals past the Reyes Adobe exit (#34).

Do you offer same-day veneers consultations?

Yes. We do digital smile design in-house using iTero and a cosmetic mock-up so you can see the proposed result before any enamel preparation.

Do you take HMO dental plans?

We are primarily a PPO and fee-for-service practice. If your plan is an HMO, our Smile Care Plan offers a transparent monthly alternative that most Calabasas families find comparable.

Is the office accessible? I park at The Commons and walk — can I do that here?

We have free on-site parking directly in front of the building — most patients prefer to drive in. The building has elevator access to our second-floor suite.

Ready to book your visit from Calabasas?

Same-day appointments available. We confirm within the hour during business days.
