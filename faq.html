Frequently Asked Questions

The quick answers
our patients ask most

Insurance, financing, emergencies, first visits, and everything in between. Still have a question? Call (818) 706-6077 — we answer the phone.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes. We welcome new patients of all ages. Book online or call (818) 706-6077 — new-patient paperwork is completed digitally before your visit, so you can fill it out from your phone at home.

Which dental insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major PPO dental plans and will file claims on your behalf. For HMO patients or those without insurance, our in-house Smile Care Plan starts at $35/month and includes cleanings, exams, and discounts on treatment.

Do you offer same-day emergency appointments?

Yes. We reserve time every day for urgent care. Call (818) 706-6077 before 10 AM and we will fit you in — broken teeth, lost fillings, toothaches, and trauma are all handled same-day when possible. Learn more about emergency dentistry.

How much does a dental implant cost?

Implant pricing depends on your specific case — single-tooth, multi-tooth, and full-arch cases all price differently. Consultations are free and you leave with an exact quote. Most implant cases qualify for 0% financing through CareCredit or LendingClub. See our implant services.

Do you treat children?

Yes. We treat patients from their first cleaning onward. We offer family appointment blocks so siblings can be seen back-to-back, digital low-radiation x-rays, and nitrous oxide for kids with dental anxiety.

What financing options are available?

We partner with CareCredit (0% APR promotional periods), LendingClub (fixed-rate installment loans), and Alphaeon (extended promotional plans up to 24 months). Our in-house Smile Care Plan is an alternative for patients without insurance. Full financing options.

Do you offer sedation dentistry?

Yes. We offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas), oral conscious sedation, and IV sedation depending on the procedure and your comfort level. All options are monitored by licensed providers. See our sedation options.

How long is the drive from Calabasas, Westlake Village, or Malibu?

Calabasas is 6 minutes east on the 101. Westlake Village is 8 minutes west. Thousand Oaks is 12 minutes. Malibu is about 20 minutes north via Kanan Road. Oak Park is 10 minutes over Kanan. See directions & map.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring a photo ID, your dental insurance card, and a list of any medications you take. New-patient forms are digital — you will receive a secure link after booking to complete them from your phone. Plan to arrive 10 minutes early.

Is there parking at the office?

Yes. Free on-site surface parking directly in front of the building. The office is on the second floor with elevator access.

How do I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Call (818) 706-6077 or reply to your appointment confirmation text. We appreciate at least 24 hours notice when possible so we can offer your slot to another patient.

Do you offer Invisalign for teens?

Yes. We use the Invisalign Teen product with compliance indicators and replacement aligners for lost or damaged trays. Treatment is tracked digitally between visits. Invisalign details.

How long do veneers last?

Porcelain veneers typically last 10 to 15 years or longer with proper care — regular cleanings, avoiding grinding habits, and wearing a night guard if recommended. Our digital design process ensures a precise fit that minimizes wear. Learn about our veneer process.

What if I have dental anxiety?

Tell us when you book and we will schedule extra time. We offer nitrous oxide (wears off in minutes), oral sedation, and IV sedation. Our team is specifically trained to work with anxious patients calmly and without pressure — there is no such thing as a silly question or a patient we have not worked with before.

Still have a question? Just call us.

We answer the phone during business hours. Same-day appointments are often available.
