A single practice for every dental need

Thousand Oaks is our largest neighboring market — thousands of families, a mature population, and a strong preference for private, doctor-owned practices over corporate chains. Our office fits that preference: two doctors, same family, three generations of craft.

Thousand Oaks patients typically find us through one of three paths: a recommendation from an existing patient, a cosmetic case (veneers or a full smile redesign), or a complex restorative case (implants, full-mouth rehab, failing older dentistry). Because we do everything in-house — including surgical implant placement, bone grafting where needed, and full-arch cases — patients rarely get bounced between offices during treatment.

The drive from Thousand Oaks is twelve minutes east on the 101 outside commute hours. Many patients combine a morning appointment with a stop at the Oaks Mall or Civic Arts Plaza on the way back.

Getting here from Thousand Oaks

From Thousand Oaks, the office is a straightforward drive east on the 101 — about 12 minutes outside of rush hour. Many patients also combine a visit with errands at the Oaks Mall or Civic Arts Plaza on the way back.

  • From the Oaks Mall / Civic Arts Plaza: take the 101 east for about 8 miles, exit Reyes Adobe Road (#34), turn right on Roadside Drive.
  • From North Thousand Oaks / Hidden Valley: take Lynn Road south to Thousand Oaks Boulevard, merge onto the 101 east, exit Reyes Adobe.
  • From Newbury Park: take the 101 east for roughly 12 miles, exit Reyes Adobe.

Free on-site parking. Elevator access to the second-floor suite.

Quick answers

Questions from Thousand Oaks patients

How long is the drive from Thousand Oaks to your office?

Approximately 12 minutes eastbound on the 101 outside commute hours. The Reyes Adobe exit (#34) delivers you within a block of the office.

Do you do All-on-4 or full-arch implant cases in-house?

Yes. Full-arch implant cases are one of our specialties. We do the CT planning, surgical placement, and final prosthetic restoration in the same practice — typically one doctor from the first consult through the final smile.

Do you accept my Thousand Oaks PPO plan?

We accept most major PPO plans in the region. Call (818) 706-6077 with your plan name or send a photo of your insurance card and we will verify benefits before your first visit.

Do you offer financing for larger cases?

Yes — we partner with CareCredit, LendingClub, and Alphaeon Credit for longer-term financing, and we offer in-house Smile Care Plan memberships for patients without insurance. See our Financing page for details.

Ready to book your visit from Thousand Oaks?

Same-day appointments available. We confirm within the hour during business days.
