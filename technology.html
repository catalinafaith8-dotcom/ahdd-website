The Tools Behind Every Perfect Outcome

Each technology we've chosen serves a clear purpose: faster diagnosis, more accurate treatment, and a more comfortable experience for you.

AI-Powered
VideaAI Diagnostics

Artificial Intelligence on Every X-Ray

VideaAI analyzes your X-rays in real time using machine learning trained on millions of dental images. Findings are overlaid directly onto your scan so you see exactly what we see — nothing hidden, nothing missed.

  • Detects cavities, bone loss, and calculus with higher consistency
  • Catches issues between teeth that are easy to miss on visual exam
  • Visual overlay reviewed side-by-side with your doctor, chairside
  • Applied at every single visit — not an optional add-on
Experience AI diagnostics
Advanced Diagnostics
3D CBCT Imaging

See What X-Rays Simply Can't Show

Traditional X-rays show two dimensions. Our in-house CBCT scanner creates a full three-dimensional map of your teeth, jawbone, nerves, and sinuses — in a single 15-second scan, right here in our office.

  • Reveals bone density, nerve locations, and anatomy invisible on flat films
  • Critical for precise implant placement and surgical treatment planning
  • Lower radiation than a full series of traditional periapical X-rays
  • Results reviewed chairside with your doctor immediately
Schedule a consultation
Digital Impressions
iTero Element Scanner

Digital Impressions in Minutes — No Goop

Our iTero intraoral scanner replaces messy impression trays with a precise 3D digital model — captured in minutes. A small wand is passed over your teeth to build a complete, permanent digital record of your dentition.

  • No trays, no gag reflex, no mess — just a small handheld wand
  • Complete 3D model of your entire dentition in under 5 minutes
  • Precision fit for crowns, veneers, retainers, and aligners
  • Digital scans stored permanently in your patient record

Invisalign Outcome Simulator

See Your New Smile Before You Commit

The iTero Outcome Simulator uses your own 3D scan to generate a real preview of your post-Invisalign smile — side-by-side with your current one. Make your decision with confidence, before a single aligner is made.

  • Real-time visualization of your projected Invisalign results
  • Side-by-side comparison with your current smile
  • Built directly from your personal 3D scan — not a stock model
  • Available at your complimentary consultation

Intraoral Cameras

See Every Detail of Your Smile in HD

Our pen-sized intraoral cameras capture crystal-clear, magnified images of every tooth — projected live onto your screen. You see exactly what your doctor sees, making every diagnosis transparent and every recommendation easy to understand.

  • High-definition live video of every tooth and surface
  • Identifies cracks, wear, and issues invisible to the naked eye
  • Images saved to your chart for progress tracking over time

Laser-Precise Gum Treatment
