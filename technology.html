VideaAI Diagnostics

Artificial Intelligence
on Every X-Ray

VideaAI analyzes your X-rays in real time using machine learning trained on millions of dental images. Findings are overlaid directly onto your scan so you see exactly what we see — nothing hidden, nothing missed.

Applied at every single visit. Not an optional add-on. The standard of care we hold ourselves to for every patient who walks through our door.

Advanced Diagnostics
3D CBCT Imaging

See What X-Rays Simply Can't Show

Traditional X-rays show two dimensions. Our in-house CBCT scanner creates a full three-dimensional map of your teeth, jawbone, nerves, and sinuses — in a single 15-second scan, right here in our office. Critical for precise implant placement, surgical planning, and catching what flat films miss entirely.

  • Full 3D anatomy — bone density, nerve paths, and sinus floors visible
  • Lower radiation than a full series of traditional periapical X-rays

Digital Impressions
iTero Element Scanner

Digital Impressions in Minutes — No Goop

Our iTero intraoral scanner replaces messy impression trays with a precise 3D digital model — captured in under 5 minutes with a small handheld wand. No trays, no gag reflex, no mess. A complete, permanent digital record of your dentition, precision-fit for crowns, veneers, retainers, and aligners.

  • Complete 3D model of your entire dentition in under 5 minutes
  • Digital scans stored permanently — accessible at every future visit

Why It Matters

Better Technology
Means Better Outcomes

The difference between a good diagnosis and a missed one often comes down to the tools your doctor has available. We chose not to compromise on either.

01

Catch problems earlier

3D imaging and AI diagnostics identify issues that conventional X-rays and visual exams miss — before they become costly emergencies. Early detection is always less invasive and less expensive.

02

When you can see your own 3D scan and AI overlay, treatment decisions become clear conversations — not blind trust. Transparency is built into every diagnosis.

03
