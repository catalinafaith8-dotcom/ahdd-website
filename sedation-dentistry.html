Our Sedation Options

Two Levels of Comfort & Relaxation

We offer two safe, proven sedation methods to match your level of anxiety and treatment complexity. Both are administered by experienced clinicians with your safety as the absolute priority.

Nitrous Oxide ("Laughing Gas")
A mild, inhaled sedative delivered through a small mask over your nose. You remain conscious and responsive but feel deeply relaxed and comfortable. It wears off within minutes, so you can drive home after the appointment. Perfect for mild to moderate anxiety or routine procedures.
Oral Conscious Sedation
A prescription sedative taken before your appointment. You'll be deeply relaxed — awake but in a twilight state, with little to no memory of the procedure. You will need a driver home. Ideal for patients with severe anxiety, complex procedures, or a strong gag reflex.
