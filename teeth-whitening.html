Common Questions

Teeth Whitening FAQ

How white will my teeth get?
Most patients achieve 6–10 shades brighter. Results depend on your starting shade and stain type. We give you a realistic expectation at your consultation.
How long do whitening results last?
With good oral hygiene and occasional touch-ups, results typically last 1–3 years. Limiting coffee, tea, and red wine extends results significantly.
Is professional whitening safe?
Yes — when supervised by a dental professional. We assess your enamel health and sensitivity before treatment and use formulations designed to minimize sensitivity.
Will whitening work on crowns or veneers?
No — whitening only affects natural tooth enamel. If you have existing restorations, we factor this into your treatment plan to ensure a consistent final look.
What about tooth sensitivity?
Some temporary sensitivity is common, especially in the first 24 hours. We use desensitizing agents during treatment and recommend sensitivity-reducing toothpaste beforehand.
What's the difference between in-office and take-home?
In-office delivers dramatic same-day results using high-concentration gel. Take-home uses custom trays with professional-strength gel for gradual, even brightening — ideal if you prefer flexibility.
