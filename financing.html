Common Questions

Financing FAQ

Which financing option is best for me?
It depends on your treatment cost and credit profile. CareCredit is best for flexible everyday use, Alphaeon excels for larger cosmetic cases with higher approval rates, and LendingClub is ideal for major restorations with fixed monthly payments. Our team can help you choose during your consultation.
Will applying affect my credit score?
LendingClub lets you check your rate with only a soft inquiry — no credit score impact. CareCredit and Alphaeon do a hard pull when you formally apply, but checking rates beforehand does not affect your score.
Can I use financing for cosmetic procedures?
Yes. All three of our financing partners cover cosmetic dental treatments including veneers, teeth whitening, Invisalign, and full smile makeovers. Alphaeon is specifically designed for elective aesthetic procedures.
What if I'm not approved by one lender?
Don't worry — each lender has different approval criteria. We encourage patients to try multiple options. Alphaeon in particular has higher approval rates for cosmetic procedures. Our team is also happy to help structure your treatment plan into phases.
How quickly can I start treatment after approval?
Immediately. Once approved, your credit line is active and we can schedule your first appointment the same day or next available opening. Same-day appointments are often available for urgent needs.
Do you accept insurance as well?
Absolutely. We accept most PPO insurance plans and can combine your insurance benefits with financing to minimize your out-of-pocket costs. Use our Insurance Checker on the homepage or call us to verify your coverage before your visit.
