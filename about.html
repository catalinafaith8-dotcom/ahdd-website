Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Shawn
Matian

General & Cosmetic Dentistry

As a third-generation dentist, Dr. Shawn Matian has spent nearly two decades redefining what modern dentistry can be. Now extending that vision to Agoura Hills Dental Designs, he brings his blend of artistry, precision, and technology-driven care to the Agoura Hills community — creating smiles that look beautiful, function naturally, and last for years to come.

A graduate of the Herman Ostrow School of Dentistry at USC, Dr. Matian's foundation in biomimetic and biocompatible dentistry shapes every aspect of his approach. He was one of the early pioneers of offering metal-free zirconia dental implants to the Southern California market. He believes that great dentistry should conserve what nature created, while leveraging the best of modern science to enhance aesthetics and longevity.

From full-mouth rehabilitation and dental implants to cosmetic enhancements and digital smile design, Dr. Matian's work reflects a deep respect for both detail and design. He is known for his meticulous craftsmanship, calm chairside manner, and relentless pursuit of excellence — understanding each patient's goals, anxieties, and expectations to deliver care that feels personal, not procedural.

Beyond the clinical realm, Dr. Matian is a mentor, entrepreneur, and lifelong student of innovation. Outside of dentistry, he finds balance in weight training, travel, and time spent with family — living by the same principles of discipline, curiosity, and intentionality that guide his professional life.

Meet Your Doctor

Dr. David
Matian

General & Cosmetic Dentistry

For Dr. David Matian, dentistry is both a passion and a proud family tradition. As a third-generation dentist, he grew up watching his father and older brother transform smiles and lives — an experience that shaped his belief that dentistry is about more than fixing teeth. It's about creating confidence, building relationships, and improving quality of life through precision and compassion.

A graduate of the Herman Ostrow School of Dentistry at USC, Dr. Matian is part of a new generation of clinicians who combine advanced training with a deeply personal approach to care. He offers comprehensive treatment under one roof — from preventive and cosmetic dentistry to full-mouth restorations and dental implants — using modern digital technologies to ensure precision and comfort at every step.

Known for his calm presence and exceptional attention to detail, Dr. Matian takes the time to listen, explain, and craft individualized treatment plans that align with each patient's goals and concerns. His focus is on long-term health and beauty, not quick fixes — approaching each case with the same meticulous care and empathy that have defined his family's legacy for decades.

Outside of the office, Dr. Matian enjoys basketball, fitness, and spending time with family. He brings that same energy and focus into his work, striving to make every visit a positive, confidence-building experience.
