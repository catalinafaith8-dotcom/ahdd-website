Getting Here

Directions from nearby communities

We serve patients from Agoura Hills, Calabasas, Westlake Village, Malibu, Thousand Oaks, and Oak Park.

From the 101 Freeway
Direct exit access
Take the Reyes Adobe Road exit (#34), turn south toward Agoura Road, then right on Roadside Drive. The building is on your right — free surface parking at the entrance.
From Calabasas
~6 minutes west
Head west on the 101 for approximately 4 miles and take the Reyes Adobe Road exit. Our office is the first professional building on Roadside Drive.
From Westlake Village
~8 minutes east
Take the 101 east, exit at Reyes Adobe Road, and follow the access road to Roadside Drive. Patients typically arrive 5 minutes before their appointment time for paperless check-in.
From Thousand Oaks
~12 minutes east
Take the 101 east past Westlake Village, exit at Reyes Adobe Road, and turn right to Agoura Road. We are on Roadside Drive, adjacent to Whizin's Square.
From Malibu
~20 minutes north
Take Kanan Road north from PCH for roughly 13 miles, turn right on Agoura Road, then left on Roadside Drive. A scenic inland route through the Santa Monica Mountains.
From Oak Park / Thousand Oaks
~10 minutes
Head south on Kanan Road through the hills into Agoura Hills, turn left on Agoura Road, then left on Roadside Drive. A quick, scenic drive.

Quick Answers

Common questions before your visit

Do you accept walk-ins and same-day emergencies?

Yes. We reserve time every day for urgent patients and dental emergencies. Call (818) 706-6077 and let us know you need to be seen today — we'll fit you in whenever possible, including on Fridays.

Which insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most PPO dental plans and are happy to file claims on your behalf. For specific plan confirmation, call us or use the insurance checker on our financing page. We also offer in-house financing and partner with CareCredit, LendingClub, and Alphaeon for flexible payment options.

Is there parking available?

Yes — free surface parking is available directly in front of the building and in the adjacent lot. Our entrance is on Roadside Drive.

How early should I arrive for my first appointment?

Plan to arrive 10 minutes early. New-patient paperwork is fully digital and can also be completed from your phone before you arrive — you will receive a secure link after booking.

What if I need to reschedule?

Call us at (818) 706-6077 or reply to your appointment confirmation text. We appreciate at least 24 hours notice when possible so we can offer your slot to another patient.
